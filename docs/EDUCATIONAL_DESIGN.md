## Learning Philosophy

AI-X is designed around interactive learning instead of passive content consumption.
Players engage with AI concepts through gameplay, narrative, and problem-solving activities.

## Teaching Methodology

* Learning by interaction
* Immediate feedback loops
* Retry-based understanding
* Adaptive hint support

## Educational Components

### Interactive Lessons

Lessons are delivered through in-game dialogue and interactive scenes.

### Quizzes

Two formats:

* Multiple choice questions
* Open-ended responses

### Practical Activities

Future modules will include coding practice and logic-based challenges evaluated by the AI assistant.

## AI Assistant Role

The AI assistant is designed to:

* Provide hints when the student makes mistakes
* Adapt explanations based on difficulty
* Encourage learning instead of punishment

## Educational Objective

To make Artificial Intelligence education engaging, creative, and accessible through a game-based environment.
